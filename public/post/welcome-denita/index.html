Welcome Denita Hadziabdic Guerry, our new Section Editor

May 24, 2018

Excited to announce Dr. Denita Hadziabdic Guerry as our new Section Editor who will be handling manuscripts in two sections: forest pathology and population biology.

Denita holds an MSc in Plant pathology and Ph.D. in Population Genetics, both obtained at the University of Tennessee. She is an Assistant Professor in the same University with a professional appointment of 90% Research and 10% Teaching. In research, her areas of expertise are Forest health and forest pathology, population genetics of fungal pathogens, population biology, genetic diversity and conservation of native plants.
